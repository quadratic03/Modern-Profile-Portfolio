Frequently Asked Questions

What information should I provide for a project inquiry?

To help me understand your project better, please include details about your business/organization, project goals, target audience, desired features, visual preferences, and any deadlines. The more information you provide, the more accurate my initial assessment will be.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

I typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, please indicate this in your message, and I'll do my best to get back to you as soon as possible.

Do you work with clients outside your location?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide and am experienced in remote collaboration. Through effective communication tools and regular updates, I ensure a smooth process regardless of geographic location.

What happens after I submit my inquiry?

After receiving your inquiry, I'll review the details and reach out to schedule an initial consultation. During this call or meeting, we'll discuss your project in more depth, clarify any questions, and determine if we're a good fit to work together. If we proceed, I'll provide a detailed proposal including scope, timeline, and pricing.
