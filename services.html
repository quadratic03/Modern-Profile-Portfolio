Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline for a website project varies depending on its complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex websites or web applications can take 8-12 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone payments. I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, or credit card.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and functioning optimally. These packages include regular updates, security monitoring, content changes, and technical support.

Can you help with an existing website?

Absolutely! I can help improve, redesign, or fix issues with your existing website. I'll conduct a thorough review of your current site and provide recommendations for enhancements or solutions to any problems.

Do you provide hosting services?

While I don't provide hosting services directly, I can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your needs and help set up your website on your chosen hosting platform. I'll ensure everything is properly configured for optimal performance.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I'll need information about your business, target audience, project goals, design preferences, and any specific functionality requirements. If you have existing brand guidelines, content, or visual assets, those will be helpful as well. I'll provide a detailed questionnaire to streamline this process.
